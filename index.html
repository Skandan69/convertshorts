How to convert landscape video to portrait (16:9 to 9:16)

Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok all require vertical 9:16 video. If you recorded in landscape (16:9) on a camera or screen recorder, you need to crop and reformat it before posting.

ConvertShorts makes this free and instant. Drop your landscape video, drag the crop slider to center your subject, and export a clean portrait MP4 — all without uploading anything to a server.

The same tool works in reverse: if you have a portrait video (9:16) from your phone that you want to use on YouTube or TV, switch to Portrait → Landscape mode and it will crop and convert to widescreen 16:9.

Supported input formats: MP4, MOV, WebM, MKV (most common video formats). Output: MP4 (H.264), compatible with all platforms.
