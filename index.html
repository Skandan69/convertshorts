What is 16:9 aspect ratio?

16:9 (sixteen by nine) is the standard widescreen format used by most cameras, TVs, and YouTube videos. A 1920×1080 (1080p) or 3840×2160 (4K) video is 16:9. It's also called "landscape" or "horizontal" video.

What is 9:16 aspect ratio?

9:16 (nine by sixteen) is the vertical format used by Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Snapchat. A 1080×1920 video is 9:16. It's also called "portrait" or "vertical" video.

How to convert 16:9 to 9:16 without losing quality
